Our Story

More Than a
Haircut — It's a
Standard.

The Philosophy

Built on Craft.
Driven by Detail.

Premier Cuts was founded on a single conviction: the people of Dayton, Tennessee deserve a barbershop that doesn't cut corners. Where every visit is an experience, not a transaction.

Carlos built this shop from the chair up — with modern equipment, a zero-compromise approach to cleanliness, and a team trained to listen as well as they cut.

The result is a barbershop that clients drive across the county for. Not because they have to. Because nothing else compares.

Carlos founded Premier Cuts with a vision that Dayton deserved more — more care, more craft, more consistency. His precision and patience with every client is legendary among regulars. He doesn't just cut hair; he builds trust one appointment at a time.

Senior Barber
