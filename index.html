FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

Walk-ins are always welcome — we work you in based on availability. That said, booking an appointment guarantees your time slot and barber. We recommend booking online via Booksy or StyleSeat, especially on weekends.

How long does a typical appointment take?

A standard haircut or fade takes 30–45 minutes. The Full Service Groom runs 60–75 minutes. We never rush — your cut gets the time it deserves.

Are your prices the same for all hair types?

Our listed prices are starting prices. Pricing may vary slightly depending on hair length, texture, or complexity of the service. Your barber will always communicate any adjustments before beginning — no surprises.

What if I'm not happy with my cut?

Tell us before you leave. We stand behind our work completely — if something isn't right, we fix it at no charge. Our satisfaction guarantee is unconditional.

Do you cut kids' hair?

Absolutely. Kids' cuts start at $20. We're patient and make the experience comfortable — parents are always welcome to stay right beside the chair.
