Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Premier Cuts Barbershop, Dayton, TN

Introduction

Premier Cuts Barbershop ("we," "our," or "us"), located at 455 Chickamauga Dr, Dayton, TN 37321, is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

By using our website or booking an appointment with us, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us directly.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website or book services:

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, and email address when you book an appointment or contact us through our website form.
  • Booking Information: Appointment details including date, time, preferred barber, and service type when scheduling through third-party platforms (Booksy, StyleSeat).
  • Communications: Messages or inquiries you send us via contact form, phone, or email.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on our site, collected automatically through standard web analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To confirm, manage, and follow up on your barbershop appointments
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To send appointment reminders or updates, with your consent
  • To improve our services and website experience
  • To comply with legal obligations if required

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Booking Platforms

Premier Cuts uses third-party platforms including Booksy and StyleSeat to manage online appointment scheduling. When you book through these platforms, your information is subject to their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies before booking:

  • Booksy: booksy.com/privacy-policy
  • StyleSeat: styleseat.com/privacy

We only receive the booking details necessary to prepare for and deliver your service.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Appointment records may be retained for service continuity (so we can recreate your preferred cut on future visits). You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us directly.

Photography & Social Media

We may photograph haircut results for use on our social media accounts and marketing materials. We will always ask for your verbal consent before taking photos. If you do not wish to be photographed, simply let your barber know and we will fully respect your preference. If a photo of you has already been posted and you wish for it to be removed, contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use standard cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site. This information is collected in aggregate and is not tied to any personally identifiable information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some site features may not function as intended.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly. We do provide haircut services to minors, but only with a parent or guardian present or with their explicit consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of any marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at (423) 567-0424 or visit us at 455 Chickamauga Dr, Dayton, TN 37321.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
