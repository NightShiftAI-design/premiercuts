Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  ·  Premier Cuts Barbershop, Dayton, TN

Introduction

Premier Cuts Barbershop ("we," "our," or "us"), located at 455 Chickamauga Dr, Dayton, TN 37321, is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

By using our website or booking an appointment with us, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. Questions? Contact us directly.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, and email address when you book or contact us.
  • Booking Information: Appointment details when scheduling through Booksy or StyleSeat.
  • Communications: Messages you send via contact form, phone, or email.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, and pages visited, collected automatically through standard web analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm, manage, and follow up on appointments
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • To send appointment reminders, with your consent
  • To improve our services and website
  • To comply with legal obligations if required

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Booking Platforms

When you book through Booksy or StyleSeat, your information is subject to their privacy policies. We only receive booking details necessary to prepare for and deliver your service.

Photography & Social Media

We may photograph results for our social media and marketing. We always ask for verbal consent first. If you don't wish to be photographed, let your barber know — your preference will be fully respected. To remove an existing photo, contact us and we'll act promptly.

Your Rights

  • Request access to information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

Contact us at (423) 567-0424 or 455 Chickamauga Dr, Dayton, TN 37321.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any updates.
